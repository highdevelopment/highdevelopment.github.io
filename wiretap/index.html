Wiretap Safeguards Against

HR Policy Violations

Wiretap protects against intentional or unintentional harassment, threats, intimidation or company-specific policy violations.

Compliance Issues

Set detailed policies to monitor and safeguard against very specific regulatory compliance issues for your industry.

Insider Threats

Safeguard against employees that may use an ESN to plot a company exit, garner customers, partners and staffing on your dime.

External Sharing Risks

Collaboration groups often include external stakeholders — Wiretap makes sure the wrong information doesn’t get into their hands.

IP Leaks

Occurring intentionally or unintentionally, employees may accidentally expose IP to unauthorized parties outside the organization.

Confidential Data Leaks

Safeguard critical customer and employee data like SSNs, credit cards, medical information or other sensitive information is often accidentally shared.
