What we like about this card

  • Introducory purchase offer of 0.99% p.a. for 4 months
  • 0% balance transfer for 20 months reverting to a low 12.74% p.a.
  • Extra bonus features like travel insurance, purchase insurance & Visa Platinum Concierce
  • 3 additional cardholders at no extra cost

Cons

  • Customers with an existing credit card balance on St.George cannot apply for the balance transfer.
  • CANNOT transfer loan balances - only balances from credit cards and store card

Editor Review

The St.George Vertigo Platinum delivers for customers looking for a low rate card (st George's lowest purchase rate) with extra bonus features. Offering customers a massive 0% balance transfer for 20 months making this a very nice package. This one comes with a range of Visa Platinum perks such as complimentary purchase insurances as well as travel insurance and Visa Platinum Concierge. If you don't mind paying a bit more a year on the annual fee and are really going to use the platinum features then this is a great credit card.
