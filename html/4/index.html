Our Service

CreditCard.com.au is operated as part of Creditworld Pty Ltd beginning personal finance comparison in Australia as far back as 2005. Since then lots has changed – customers expect more choice and the banks have to provide more value in order to win your business. This is reflected in the number of credit card special offers available in the market today – banks are fighting hard for you!

We’re always keen to look at new topics and investigate matters further for you – our aim is to make credit card comparison easier and easier. We can access more and more information every day, giving more decision making power to our users.

You can also make a shortlist of cards that you find interesting simply by clicking on ‘compare’, submit a question if you can’t find the information that you’re looking for. You can also add a review of a card, and help the other site users understand how you feel about it.

Check out our credit card guides and resources section for help learning about all things credit card – balance transfers, bonus points, interest free days, annual fees, revert rates, converting reward points, free flights, free insurance – and credit cards for students, pensioners or even 457 visa holders.

Visit our blog for useful news, features and reviews. There is a wide range of articles from debt management through to using credit cards for shopping and travel – and we are always keen for more stories and information. Stay in touch with what’s going on by following us on Facebook or signing up for our email newsletter. We look forward to helping you with your credit card questions.
