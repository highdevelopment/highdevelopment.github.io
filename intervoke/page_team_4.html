NIC VAN DESSEL

Senior VR specialist and Unity Coder

Nic earned a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Emergent Digital Practices at the University of Denver. He is focused on a career that encompasses the many interests in virtual reality, digital, multimedia, and electronic technologies. With a keen aptitude and passion in learning and mastering new technologies, he has acquired a strong grasp of fundamentals in virtual reality, coding in 2D and 3D environments, animation, graphic design, visual programming, audio-visual remixing, and other interactive media programs. These are new and exciting skills that equip Nic with abilities in the creation of virtual spaces and experiences to visualize, explore, and embrace approaches beyond those that are already defined and familiar. In this way, he hopes to shape the future with the use of new technologies.
