About Our Studio

INTERVOKE is a dynamic group of people who have come together to engineer custom medical training programs and applications for the medical community.

Our goal is to be the best in everything we produce and achieve scientific accuracy while presenting content in a clean interactive application while using photorealistic thought provoking visuals. We have some of the best people in the industry working for us to create unprecedented 3D animations, interactive software and application

Our team consists of 3D animators, medical illustrators, Unity code engineers, virtual reality specialists, and custom tailored physicians who have created award winning medical animations for over 20 years. We love what we do and it shows in all of the art we produce.

our creative minds

We keep our finger on the pulse of new technology. Using the most current hardware and software. We can port Virtual reality to the Vive and Oculus headsets. We have a the largest in-house triple GPU renderfarm in the US. All computers rendering using Redshift and Vray.

Our Process

“In order to explain something well, one has to be able to demonstrate its necessity.”

Aristotle

We Define

1. Our process starts to unfold as our team works closely with clients to define the project in order to determine the right approach to develop the end product.

2. Research is key to the development success of any product. Our team works closely with clinicians and physician consultants to fully define the medical concept or software purpose; whether it is for a medical device, surgical procedure or pharmaceutical drug, we will define the device, procedure or drug mechanisms of action (MOA) and/or the mechanisms of disease (MOD).

3. We define the end user is established we consult and define a “User Interface Experience” that will present your material in a clean, user friendly way.

4. Once the project’s purpose has been defined and we have client approval, we can then begin development.

We Develop

1. After we have defined the project and the end user, our team of Project Managers, Art Directors, Engineers & Animators work closely together to plan development.

2. Next, we start with visualization. Which includes storyboarding and concept design. Using standardized clean code practices for engineering software is essential in generating efficient applications that will work quickly and smoothly with high end graphics. All content will be organized, tested and implemented in a clean and simple to use interactive interface, allowing access at any time to detailed and direct individual teaching elements.

3. Content can include detailed animated pop-up menus, voice-over audio, sound effects, interactive 3D graphics, and a modern content delivery system; including corporate or institutional logos, specific color schemes, and other product information for various types of educational media.

We Deliver

1. Once development is complete we prepare for product delivery. All media can be available on smart phones, tablets, computers and rendered to photo-realistic detail and/or in real-time for virtual reality. Using Gear VR, Google Daydream, the Oculus and the Vive with optimum efficiency.

2. Our Content Delivery System (CDS ) can be viewed in interactive and cinematic modes. Viewers can simply switch back and forth between either modes by swiping a button in the program at any time allowing the user to touch the screen and learn at there own pace, interacting with the 3D graphics.
