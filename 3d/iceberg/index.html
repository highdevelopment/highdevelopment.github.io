Privacy Insights

Protecting privacy means going below the surface

Protenus is a fundamentally different way to monitor the depths of your organization and protect patient privacy in the electronic health record (EHR). Trusted by top healthcare organizations, it helps privacy teams act on obvious and hidden privacy threats.

Deeply understand how each member of your workforce accesses patient records in the EHR. Hundreds of analytics including historical clinical activity, patterns of documentation, and typical interactions between colleagues determine both appropriate and inappropriate accesses for all EHR users.

Move beyond flat snapshots of historical data, cumbersome auditing platforms that drown you in false alerts, or waiting by the phone for privacy complaints. Gain a comprehensive perspective, save time, resources, and reputation by embracing a different approach to determining and resolving breaches of privacy.

Expective simple, human-friendly explanations of suspected privacy breaches

Privacy teams are busy. They are asked to manage more with increasingly strained resources. Entrusted with protecting patients' healthcare records from misuse, these teams manage the risk posed by business associates, comminity affiliates, and ever-expanding workforces, all while operating under the watchful eye of HHS, ONC, and organizational leadership.

Adhering to HIPAA and HITECH is required, but only represents a portion of what leading institutions do to protect patient privacy. Only a comprehensive view, established through proactive monitoring that produces simple-to-understand, digestible knowledge, will deliver the necessary confidence to all stakeholders.
